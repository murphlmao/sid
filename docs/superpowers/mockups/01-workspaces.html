Workspaces tab: left rail shows an umbrella tree (~/vcs, ~/work) with their child repos and a separate "auto-detected" list of git dirs sid has noticed but you haven't yet adopted.
Right pane shows live detail for the selected repo (~/vcs/sid) — branch, ahead-count, dirty status, remote, and recent commits — plus single-key shortcuts to drill into git actions.
The "New Workspace" modal answers "how do I add a workspace?" directly: press N anywhere on this tab, fill in Name + Path, choose Repo or Umbrella, optionally describe it, Enter saves.
While the modal is open the background panes are desaturated and the starfield motion is paused — visually signalling that all input is captured by the dialog until Esc cancels or Enter commits.
